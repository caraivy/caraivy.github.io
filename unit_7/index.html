What We Do

  • 'Bee' part of the solution to bee colony collapse and fill your green space with trees, flowers and plants that bees love.

  • A garden with benefits! Pollution control, flooding protection, habitat creation and water conservation, to name a few.

  • Don't have the space to grow out? Grow up! We can turn any wall into a verticle garden -- adding beauty and a breath of fresh air to your home or work space.

  • Make your roof work for you! Conserve energy, reduce stormwater runoff and produce less greenhouse gasses. Your lungs and heating bill will thank you!
